## QUIZ SCORING LOGIC

### Scoring System:
Questions are weighted to identify the user's PRIMARY concern:

**Budget concerns trigger on:**
- Q2: "Can I actually afford this?"
- Q3: "We'll go broke trying to do this right"
- Q5: "Tight‚Äîwe need to make every dollar count"
- Q8: "Having a realistic budget plan"

**Socialization concerns trigger on:**
- Q2: "What about socialization?!"
- Q3: "My kid will be weird and friendless"
- Q6: "Pretty isolated‚Äîthis is actually a concern"
- Q8: "Knowing it won't ruin my kid socially"

**Getting Started concerns trigger on:**
- Q2: "I have no idea where to even start"
- Q3: "I'll fail them academically" OR "I'm not qualified to teach"
- Q4: "Terrified‚ÄîI'm not a teacher!"
- Q7: "I need a detailed roadmap or I'll freeze"
- Q8: "Understanding the basics without overwhelm"

**Result assignment:**
User gets assigned to whichever category had the most triggers. Tie goes to Getting Started (default path).
